### Where are these specimens from?
- All plant specimens were collected in May 2025 from the cultivated plant species outside the University of Arizona's Biological Sciences West building
- Specimens were kept at 40* F for 48 hours before collecting spectra

##### How are species identified?
- Plants are identified to the species level using PlantNet (identify.plantnet.org)

### Spectra collection:
- Spectra were collected from specific petal and sepal locations where color visible and non-visible color differences were most stark
	- I.E. When a color-gradient is visible, spectra were collected from the furthest visible ends of the color-gradient OR
	- When UV-patterning was detected, spectra were collected from regions where reflectance was highest
